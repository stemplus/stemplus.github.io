What is STEM+



Research has shown that a high percentage of Nigerian students especially in public secondary schools and universities do not get adequate exposure to laboratory experience to complement class theory. This could be attributed to poor infrastructure and over-population in public schools.

Stem+ is an interactive virtual laboratory software designed to solve this problem. It provides students with an environment to perform virtual experiments designed to be as realistic as possible using modern technology and reap the same benefits of using a physical laboratory.

Features

Risk Free

Break the rules and explore possibilities without risk to self or expensive equipment

Works offline, Download Once, Use Anytime

Unlike other Virtual Laboratories, STEM+ works offline, because we understand the challenges of cost and poor internet access especially in rural areas of Nigeria. Just download an experiment box once, and use anytime

Mobility and Ease of Setup

Experiment everywhere you go, at home, school or even in commute. It's that easy.
